# Mixing and Mastering Notes

## Gain staging

Leave headroom before the mastering chain. Avoid clipping individual channels and buses unless the distortion is intentional.

## Mix translation

Check the mix on headphones, small speakers, phone speakers, and a reference playback system. Compare against two or three reference tracks in the same genre.

## Mastering checklist

- Verify integrated loudness and true peak targets for the release platform.
- Compare tonal balance against references.
- Check mono compatibility.
- Listen quietly and loudly.
- Export a versioned master and keep notes about limiter, EQ, saturation, and stereo processing choices.

## AI assistance targets

Use the knowledge base for recall: plugin manuals, notes about previous mixes, reference-track observations, arrangement decisions, and troubleshooting patterns.
